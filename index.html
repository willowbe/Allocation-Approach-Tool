Welcome to the Allocation Approach Decision Support Tool!

This tool will help you identify allocation approaches that may be appropriate for your fishery based on your goals and the challenges you face. Feel free to hover over any [?]Yep, just like that! boxes you see for a quick definition.

Please note that this tool is intended to help generate ideas to inform decision-making, and should *not* be used as a replacement for systematic, inclusive, participatory decision-making processes. Neither EDF nor the authors takes responsibility for the results of the use of this tool.

This tool is for internal use only.
